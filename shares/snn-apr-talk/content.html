Annual Progress Report on Brain-inspired and Cognitive Computing

Shi Gu, Published on Jul 15, 2025

Event: Invited APR Talk at VALSE 2025

Date: June 6, 2025

Location: Zhuhai

Abstract: This is the APR talk on VALSE 2025. "Brain-inspired and Cognitive Computing" integrates neuroscience and artificial intelligence, aiming to enhance the intelligence of computational systems by simulating and learning from the cognitive mechanisms of the human brain. This annual progress report will systematically review the latest research achievements in neurally inspired algorithms, cognitive modeling, and brain-like architectures, showcasing the field's trend towards multidisciplinary fusion and its future applications.
